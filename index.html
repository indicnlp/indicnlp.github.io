Linguistic data platform and computing tools for the regional languages of India

WHO ARE WE

We have built language models for Tamil, Malayalam and Bangla. We hope to build for many more indigenous languages.

We have ambitious goal of building a series of tools for indigenous languages, such

Data Tagging

Android/iOS App for Data collection, Tagging

Translation

Translation system across Indic and other languages

STT and TTS

Speech-to-Text and Text-to-Speech to help converse with the computer

WHY OPEN DATA?

We aim to collect datasets that focus on regional indian languages and keep them in the public domain for everyone forever. There are hundreds of regional language and numerous dialects in India. Availability of datasets in the open domain encourages and helps you, I and everyone to learn and build AI applications.
